Homework Frosting

Quickly create a list of your assignments in Microsoft Teams:
  1. Go to https://teams.microsoft.com
  2. Sign in, if necessary
  3. Click on Assignments in the far left navigation bar
  4. Click the cupcake icon in the top right
  5. Wait about ten seconds and the report will be displayed

Options

To filter out assignments you don't want to see, enter words or phrases below, one per line. For example, if you never want to see assignments containing "extra credit" or by the teacher Mr. Macinerny, then type each on its own line. Phrases are not sensitive to upper/lower case.



Homework Frosting can send your assignments to myHomework so that you can see assignments, on your phone. You will need a key from https://myhomeworkapp.com/settings/homework-external-apps.




  


Privacy Policy

The makers of Homework Frosting do not collect or store your personal data. We never see your personal data and therefore can't share it, even if we were asked to. However, Homework Frosting works with other companies that DO store your personal information, including your homework data, and these companies have their own Privacy Policies. Currently, these companies are Microsoft and, (if you enable it) myHomework.

Your usage of the Homework Frosting Chrome Extension is managed by Google. They have their own Privacy Policy. Google does not tell us any personal information about who installs Homework Frosting.
